# Technical Assessment – Data Analyst & Visualization

## Objective
Analyze the provided dataset and deliver structured analytical insights from both technical and business perspectives.

You are expected to demonstrate:
- SQL proficiency
- Analytical reasoning
- Business understanding
- Data visualization skills

---

## Deliverables

1. **SQL Queries**
   - Provide all queries used in your analysis.
   - Queries must be well-structured and readable.

2. **Analytical Summary (PDF)**
   - Executive summary (1–3 pages)
   - Key findings
   - Identified issues or risks
   - Strategic recommendations

3. **Tableau Public Dashboard**
   - Publish your dashboard to Tableau Public
   - Submit the public link

---

## Time Limit
You have **3 calendar days** to complete this assessment.

---

## Guidelines
- You may use any SQL engine.
- For BI tool, Tableau Public is mandatory for submission.
- Assumptions must be clearly stated.

---

## Evaluation Criteria
We will evaluate based on:
- SQL depth and correctness
- Logical thinking
- Clarity of communication
- Business relevance of insights
- Visualization effectiveness
